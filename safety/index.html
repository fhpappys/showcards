Community Safety Guidelines

Our DIY venue is a community space built on mutual respect, care, and accountability. These guidelines exist to keep shows fun, loud, and safe for everyone.

Respect & Safer Space

  • Zero tolerance for harassment, assault, or hate speech
  • Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, ableism, and intimidation are not welcome
  • Respect people’s boundaries, identities, and pronouns

Crowd Safety

  • Moshing is consensual — look out for each other
  • Pick people up if they fall
  • No pushing people who aren’t participating
  • Keep exits, stairs, and hallways clear at all times
  • Naloxone (Narcan) and basic first aid on site when possible

What’s Not Allowed

  • No weapons of any kind
  • No outside food or drink, including alcohol
  • No hate symbols on clothing or gang colors
  • Illicit drugs
Entry may be denied or revoked to keep the space safe.

Accessibility & Inclusion

  • We aim to make the space as accessible as possible
  • Seating and quiet space available upon request
  • Sliding-scale or donation-based entry whenever we can

If There’s a Problem

  • Find a safety volunteer or organizer
  • We prioritize harm reduction, de-escalation, care, and community accountability
  • Anyone causing harm may be removed from the space
Police are a last resort and only used in emergencies.

Community Agreement

By attending events here, you agree to respect these guidelines and help keep the space safe for everyone.

Take care of each other.
